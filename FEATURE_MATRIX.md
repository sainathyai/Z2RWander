Ask yourself these questions:

### 1. How many developers will use this?
- **1-3:** Phase 1-2
- **4-10:** Phase 2-3
- **10+:** Phase 3-5

### 2. What's your team's technical level?
- **Expert:** Phase 1 might be enough
- **Mixed:** Phase 2-3 recommended
- **Junior-heavy:** Phase 4-5 (needs great docs)

### 3. How often do you onboard new developers?
- **Rarely:** Phase 1-2
- **Quarterly:** Phase 2-3
- **Monthly+:** Phase 4-5

### 4. What's your timeline?
- **Need it now:** Phase 1 (2 weeks)
- **1-2 months:** Phase 2-3
- **Can wait 2+ months:** Phase 4-5

### 5. Do you deploy to Kubernetes?
- **No:** Skip Phase 6
- **Yes, but not locally:** Skip Phase 6
- **Yes, need local k8s:** Include Phase 6

### 6. What's your budget?
- **< $30K:** Phase 1-2
- **$30-60K:** Phase 2-3
- **$60-90K:** Phase 3-5
- **$90K+:** All phases
